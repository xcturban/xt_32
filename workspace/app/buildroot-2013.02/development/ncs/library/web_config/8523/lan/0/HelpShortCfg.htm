Alarm mode：Used to configure alarm input terminal of the action.
Output mode：Used to configure alarm output terminal of the action.
Tamper alarm：Used to configure whether to enable the tamper alarm.
Tamper alarm linkage：Used to configure the tamper alarm linkage of output when triggered.
Tamper alarm mode：Used to configure tamper alarm signal.
Sound alarm：Enable noise alarm.
Sound alarm volume：Set volume of noise alarm.
Sound alarm time：Set time of noise alarm.
